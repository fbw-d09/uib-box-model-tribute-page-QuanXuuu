Shinzō Abe

"He was the longest-serving prime minister in Japanese history."

  • 1954 - Born in Shinjuku, Tokyo
  • 2006 - 2007 First term as prime minister
  • 2012 - 2014 Second term as prime minister
  • 2014 - 2017 Third term as prime minister
  • 2017 - 2020 Fourth term as prime minister
  • 2022 - Assassination

“I would like to emphasise today to the people of India that the Japanese people stand ready to work together with the Indian people so that this spirit of tolerance becomes the leading principle of this century…I come before you on behalf of the citizens of another democracy that is equally representing Asia, to speak to you about my views on the future of Japan and India”

--Shinzō Abe
